Bastien Tessanne aka Boem, I'm a graphic design student who recently started drawing typography, I don't see myself as a typographer but as an enthusiast who makes typography for fun and uses it for personal projects. I put my fonts on open access until I consider them finalized enough to market, so jump at the chance before I become the best typographer in the world and the prices go up. You can of course use all my fonts without changing their shape, the only consideration is to mention me in the project or to mention me if they are used on social networks, here is my instagram. Instagram: @boem_c4
